About Dashmetry

Dashmetry, a PC-exclusive take on the Geometry Dash principle, will put your reflexes to the test. Challenge every official stage or let your imagination run wild with the built-in level editor for hours of platforming fun. From simple warm-ups to nearly impossible gauntlets, all official levels are designed to test your timing and precision. Each stage is precisely synchronized with its soundtrack, making every jump feel like a part of the beat.

Build, Share, and Compete

The level builder allows you to create your own obstacle courses, experiment with layouts, and add music for a personalized touch. Share your masterpieces and challenge others to outperform your designs.
